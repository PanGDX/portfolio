### TLDR;

- Leveraged ChatGPT for mass translation of Chinese web novels into English.
- Developed custom pre-processing and post-processing scripts to ensure consistent translation of character names and specific terms.
- The system significantly reduces the manual effort required for translation, though human oversight is still needed for quality assurance.
- Demonstrates the power of LLMs in localization and content adaptation tasks.
